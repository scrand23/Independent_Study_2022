The 2nd portion of this study utilized climate model ouput from CESM1 in two, 30-year time periods: 2011-2040 and 2071-2100. Monthly, daily, and 6-hourly outputs for
the following variables:
  -FSNS
  -PRECL
  -PRECT
  -PSL
  -QBOT
  -RELHUM
  -TMQ
  -TREFHT
  -TREFHTMN
  -TREFHTMX
  -UBOT
  -VBOT
  -WSPDSRFAV
  -SWPDSRFMX
  
Model ouput was limited to a domain of 240E-270E longitude and 20N-60N latitude.
